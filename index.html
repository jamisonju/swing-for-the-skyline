About the Event

A luxury charity golf experience.
A community giving back.

A luxury charity golf experience hosted by Justin Jamison and Geoff Moore, presented by ONE Fort Myers. An elevated golf outing bringing together Southwest Florida real estate, development, business, and lifestyle leaders.

Most events in Southwest Florida slow down during the summer. We saw that as an opportunity, not a reason to wait. With back to school season around the corner, August felt like the right time to bring the community together in support of local kids and families who need it most.

This is a curated day at Country Club of Naples built around great golf, meaningful connections, and real impact for kids heading back to school in Lee County.

Limited to 72 golfers. Every spot has a purpose. Every sponsor has a seat at the table.

Meet Your Hosts

Justin Jamison
& Geoff Moore

A day built around golf, community, and giving back to Southwest Florida.

Justin Jamison

Luxury Real Estate Advisor | SERHANT.

Justin Jamison is one of the more recognizable names in Southwest Florida real estate, known for his relationship driven approach and his personal brand, NotQuite7FootRealtor. Standing 6 foot 10, Justin first made his mark as a Division I basketball player and professional baseball player before transitioning into luxury real estate. Today, he helps buyers, investors, and families navigate the Southwest Florida market across Fort Myers, Cape Coral, Naples, and beyond. Through Swing for the Skyline, Justin wanted to create an event that brings together business leaders and the local community around a meaningful cause.

Geoff Moore

REALTOR® | SERHANT.

Before real estate, Geoff Moore spent years in the professional golf world as both a teaching professional and competitive player, competing throughout North America in mini tour, Web.com, and PGA sanctioned events. His background in golf and deep knowledge of Southwest Florida have helped him build strong relationships throughout the local community and luxury market. Geoff also serves on the board of a nonprofit supporting at risk youth, making this event a natural extension of causes he has long cared about. Known for his approachable personality and first class client experience, Geoff brings both authenticity and passion to the event.

Together, Justin and Geoff created Swing for the Skyline to bring people together around great golf, meaningful relationships, and a shared commitment to supporting local kids and families in Southwest Florida.

Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is the dress code?

Country Club of Naples is a private club and proper golf attire is required. Collared shirts, golf shorts or slacks, and golf shoes are encouraged. Denim, gym attire, and overly casual clothing are not permitted.

Can guests attend the cocktail hour and awards if they are not golfing?

Yes. Guests are welcome to attend the post-round cocktail hour and awards celebration. Please note guest spots are subject to capacity and should be confirmed with the event team in advance.

What is included with registration?

Golf registration includes tournament play, cart access, practice facilities, on-course refreshments, tournament gifting, cocktail hour access, and the awards reception following play.

What if I register as an individual and not a full team?

Individual golfers are welcome. We will pair individual players with other guests and sponsors to create balanced foursomes and a great experience throughout the day.

Is the backpack drive donation required?

No. The backpack drive is completely optional but strongly encouraged for anyone who would like to contribute additional support to local students and families in Lee County heading into the school year.

How are sponsorships confirmed?

Sponsorship opportunities are reserved once a commitment is confirmed with the event team. Payment is required to officially secure and hold your sponsorship placement.

When will golfers and sponsors receive event details?

Registered golfers and sponsors will receive confirmation details, arrival information, and event updates leading up to tournament week. Sponsor branding and coordination information will be provided separately.

Are custom sponsorship packages available?

Yes. If none of the listed tiers fit your goals or budget, reach out and we will build something together.
